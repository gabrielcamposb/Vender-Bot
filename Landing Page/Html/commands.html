Vender Bot Commands

!help

Gives you a list of all available commands and how to use them.

!profile

Shows your profile or that of another member you choose.

!rank

Shows your current rank or that of another desired member.

!leaderboard

Shows the server level leaderboard.

!time

Show how long you have been in voice chat. Voice chat also gives experience and credits.

!edit

Edit your profile, rank, and levelup card.

!reputation

Give a reputation point to someone.

!daily

Get your daily credits.

!balance

Check your current balance.

!give

Transfer a desired amount of your credits to another member.

!blackjack

Play a round of blackjack with Vender or friends.

!steam

Search for a steam user, get information about a game and much more.

!server

Get information about your server. Icon, owner, members, ID, ...

!role

Get information about a role. Color, ID, ...

!connect

Let Vender connect to a voice channel.

!play

Play or queue a song.

!queue

Display the queued songs.

!playing

Check what song is currently playing.

!pause

Pause the music.

!resume

Resume the music.

!skip

Skip the current song.

!volume

Change the volume of the music.

!stop

Let Vender clear the queue and disconnect.

!repeat

Let Vender repeat the current queue.

!card

Receive from Vender a random card of one of the most popular anime characters.

!roll

Try your luck and trade the last card received for a new one.

!trade

Trade or sell character cards with other users
